About us

Located in the centre of Millennium City, Yadunandan Multi Speciality Clinic Gurugram, is the answer to all your day to day healthcare needs. We have a multi-speciality outdoor department with a range of highly qualified professionals from premiere Institutions. Our areas of Specialist Services include: General Medicine, Paediatrics, Gynaecology & Obstetrics, Development Paediatrics etc.

We have a day care facility for day cases, first-aid facilities, vitals and B.P recording.

We lay great emphasis on the quality and time devoted to attending to each patient at our clinic.
